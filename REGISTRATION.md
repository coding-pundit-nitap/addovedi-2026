## 🔐 Phase 1: Account Enlistment & Credentials

To begin, participants must create a secure credential hash to access the ADDOVEDI main systems.

### Steps:
1. Open the **ADDOVEDI ID PORTAL** by clicking the **Official Portal** navigation links or profile indicators in the UI.
2. In the Portal modal, toggle the tab to **ENLIST NEW PLAYER**.
3. Provide the required core account credentials:
   - **Player Full Name**: Your real name or visual callsign.
   - **Secure Email Address**: Used as your login identifier.
   - **Mobile Number**: Primary communications channel.
   - **Access Code / Password**: Used to lock your access credentials.
4. Click **COMMENCE ENLISTMENT**. This registers your basic account locally.

> **Note**: If you already have credentials, use the **ACCESS CREDENTIALS** tab, input your email and password, and click **INITIALIZE LINK** to login.

## 🌐 Phase 2: Global Participant Profile & Unique ID Generation

Once authenticated, your profile must be fully verified and linked to academic records to claim your Unique ADDOVEDI Passport ID.

### Steps:
1. After successful login or enlisting, the portal automatically loads **STAGE 2: GLOBAL PARTICIPANT RECORD**.
2. Complete the academic profile form fields:
   - **Biological Gender** (Select Male, Female, or Other/Secure)
   - **Date of Birth**
   - **College / University** (Full institution name)
   - **Department / Stream** (e.g. Computer Science, Electronics)
   - **Academic Year / Semester** (1st Year to 4th Year)
   - **State & City**
   - **Emergency Contact Number** (Guardian contact details)
3. **Choose Player Avatar**: Pick one of the four cyberpunk gamer avatar classes that matches your specialization:
   - `SPECTER_X9` (Stealth Operative)
   - `CYPHER_NET` (Core Protocol Infiltrator)
   - `NEBULA_V` (Quantum Singularity Recon)
   - `AEGIS_SENTINEL` (Heavy Mecha Vanguard)
4. Click **COMPILE & GENERATE ID**.
5. The system will sync your record and generate a unique **ADDOVEDI ID** (Format: `ADV26-XXXX`), along with an active digital ID passport card and security QR code.

## 🎮 Phase 3: Event Arena Enlistment

Once you possess a valid ADDOVEDI ID, you are authorized to register for tech fest arena events and competitions.

### Steps:
1. Click **ENTER ARENA** on your ID Card dashboard or navigate to `/event` (the Event Arena pages).
2. Choose an event category (e.g., Coding sprints, Robotics showdowns, Web design arenas).
3. Select the event you want to join and open its details window.
4. Click **REGISTER** inside the event panel.
5. The system automatically fetches your logged-in session data and autofills your **Leader Details**:
   - **Leader Name** (Autofilled from Phase 1)
   - **Leader Unique ID / G-ID** (Autofilled from Phase 2)
   - **Leader Phone / Comms** (Autofilled from Phase 1)
6. Fill in the specific Team details:
   - **Team Name / Callsign**: The name of your team.
   - **Team Size**: Select from `1` (Solo) to `5` members depending on the rules of the event.
7. Click **INITIATE REGISTRATION PROTOCOL**.
8. Once submitted, your slot will be officially registered. You can verify your active registrations at any time by opening the **ADDOVEDI ID PORTAL** and checking the **REGISTERED EVENTS** list on the right-hand panel of your dashboard.
